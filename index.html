The best experts, at your premises at lunch time, for free!

How does it work?

Find a speaker

Find a local expert with a topic of interest for you. Contact him/her to arrange for a date.

Buy him/her lunch

The expert brings knowledge, you bring food! ;)

Spend a good time

Take advantage of the presence of the expert to explore a topic in depth and assess its benefits for your company.

Want to share your expertise?


Do you have knownledge that you want to share with others? Present a Brown Bag Lunch!
You will:

  • expand your professional network, and publicize yourself
  • share your enthusiam with others!

To make yourself known, add yourself to our github site, or send details by email.
